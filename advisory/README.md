# SMARTHAUS Advisory

Most AI consulting starts with a tool recommendation and ends with a pilot that nobody can explain.

We do the opposite. Every SMARTHAUS engagement — from a two-day express assessment to a multi-year retainer — is underpinned by **Mathematical Autopsy (MA)**, our construction discipline for building AI systems that are traceable, auditable, and deterministic.

That means when we tell you to invest in something, we can show you *why* — mathematically. When we tell you to stop, we can show you that too.

## Why This Matters

The AI industry has a failure problem. The majority of AI projects never make it to production. The ones that do often can't explain how they work, why they made a decision, or what happens when conditions change.

The root cause isn't technology. It's methodology. Teams build AI the way they build software — iteratively, experimentally, hoping the model will "figure it out." That works for prototypes. It doesn't work for systems your business depends on.

**SMARTHAUS exists to solve this.** We apply mathematical rigor to every advisory engagement so that outcomes are predictable, decisions are defensible, and your investment is protected.

## What Makes Us Different

### Mathematics, Not Opinions

Every recommendation we make passes through Mathematical Autopsy — a five-phase discipline that converts business intent into formal structure, validates assumptions against evidence, and produces guarantees you can hold us to.

Other firms give you slide decks. We give you math.

### Deterministic Delivery

For any given set of inputs, constraints, and context, our process produces the same recommendations. That's not a philosophy — it's an operating standard. Your outcomes don't depend on which consultant had a good day.

### Proof Before Investment

We don't ask you to trust us. We ask you to verify. Every engagement produces explicit success criteria, measurable checkpoints, and auditable decision logs. If the evidence doesn't support moving forward, we'll be the first to tell you.

### Right-Sized for Real Organizations

We work with organizations that have 10 to 100 people — not enterprises with unlimited budgets. Our engagements are scoped to deliver meaningful results without requiring you to bet the company.
