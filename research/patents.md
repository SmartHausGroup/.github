# Patents

**Three filed. More in flight.**

---

SMARTHAUS files patents on the methods that make our substrate work. Not on the AI category generally — on the specific mathematical techniques that produce reproducible, auditable, regulator-defensible AI systems.

## Filed

### Resonant Field Storage (RFS)
**Filed: December 2025**

Memory as a resonant field. Information is stored as wave patterns in a 4D Fourier field; retrieval works via matched-filter probing rather than brute-force vector cosine. RFS handles semantic structure (who did what to whom) and out-of-distribution rejection (the system knows what it does not know) in ways that flat embedding-cosine cannot.

The substrate underneath our long-horizon memory and the centerpiece of the [Princeton research collaboration](./princeton.md).

### Universal Control Plane (UCP)
**Filed: April 2026**

Runtime governance through an admission gate. Every AI action — model call, tool invocation, agent step, data access — passes through a policy gate before execution and produces a cryptographically signed record of the authority chain that admitted it. Nothing executes that hasn't been admitted. The record is portable, replayable, and admissible.

The mechanism that resolves the **PROVE** and **PREVENT** failures in our [Six Failures analysis](../six-failures/).

### Mathematical Autopsy (MA)
**Filed: April 2026**

A development discipline — and the engine that enforces it — that produces software whose claims are backed by formal mathematical artifacts (lemmas, invariants, notebook proofs, signed receipts, CI gates) rather than tests and documentation. MA is what makes the rest of the SMARTHAUS substrate buildable. It is also a standalone methodology that customer engineering teams can adopt.

The discipline behind every SMARTHAUS product. See [Mathematical Autopsy →](../mathematical-autopsy/).

## In flight

Additional patents are in preparation covering downstream methods — the encoder bridge between NME and RFS, the multi-tenant authority topology in UCP, and specific composition patterns that emerge from running Mathematical Autopsy at scale.
