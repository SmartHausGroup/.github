# Mathematical Thesis

**The formal mathematical foundation underneath the SMARTHAUS substrate.**

---

## What it is

The SMARTHAUS Mathematical Thesis is the document that formalizes what we are building and why it works. It is not marketing. It is not a whitepaper. It is the mathematical specification that all SMARTHAUS products are derived from and tested against.

The current edition is **v8**, May 2026.

## What it covers

The thesis develops the mathematical objects underneath our substrate and proves the properties our products depend on. Major sections include:

- **The field formalism** — the 4D Fourier field that underpins Resonant Field Storage; its operator definitions, its evolution dynamics, the role-swap discrimination property, and the controlled-dynamics admission criteria.
- **Encoder–field coupling** — the mapping `Φ_frame: text-frame → X_assoc state at an anchor μ` that bridges the Nota Memoria Engine encoder into the field, with full lemma stack (L7 through L12).
- **The seven by-construction properties** — Reproducible, Traceable, Explainable, Auditable, Replayable, Falsifiable, Verifiable — defined as mathematical obligations rather than aspirational labels.
- **The Mathematical Autopsy operating model** — the 9-phase pipeline formalized as governance over mathematical artifacts (lemmas, invariants, scorecards, signed receipts).
- **Governance algebra** — how admission gates, authority chains, and signed receipts compose into a runtime governance plane (the formalism underneath UCP).

Each section is paired with the executable verification artifacts that confirm the claims hold: notebooks, invariants, scorecards. The thesis is a specification; the codebase is the proof.
